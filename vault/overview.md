# Overview

Lagoon vaults are based on the ERC7540 standard both for deposits and redemptions. They are built in solidity and are working on EVM compatible chains. They have been [audited](../resources/audits.md) by Nethermind and accessible on deployable on several chains.

You can find more explanations on how they work in the following sections:

* [Deposit and redemptions flows](deposit-and-withdraw-flows.md)
* [Vault valuation](vault-valuation.md)
* [Fees](fees.md)
* [Roles inside the vault](roles-and-capacities/)
