# Lagoon Vault Architecture

A typical Lagoon vault architecture is made of an ERC7540 vault and a curating solution. Lagoon is un-opinionated on the curating solution to use.&#x20;

Here you can see two cases, one made of a Safe with a Zodiac Role Modifier and one with a MPC solution.
